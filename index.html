About
From the Garage to Your Garage

Built Because I Needed Them

BenchBuilt3D started the way most good ideas do - out of frustration. I was sick of losing bolts during engine jobs, so I designed a tray that actually keeps everything organized and labeled. Then I built a test light holder because mine kept rolling off the bench.

Every product starts as something I need in my own shop, gets designed from scratch, test-printed in PETG on Bambu Lab printers, and put through real use before I'll sell it to anyone else.

Based out of Ohio. One-man shop. Every item printed, inspected, and packed by hand.
